About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student at North China Electric Power University (NCEPU), majoring in Computer Science.

My research interests focus on Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs). I am dedicated to exploring feasible pathways for advancing model intelligence amidst the diminishing returns of scaling. Specifically, I focus on extending the capability boundaries of models under data-scarce conditions, aiming to achieve robust self-evolution without heavy reliance on external supervision.
